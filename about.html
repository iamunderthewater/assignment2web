About Us


Pioneering a Path of Innovation in Mobility Since 1996

The intriguing narrative of AZoom Car Rental unfolds against the vibrant canvas of 1996, a year that marked a paradigm shift in the transportation industry. Founded by the forward-thinking Siddiq family, AZoom embarked on a journey to redefine the conventional car rental experience, ultimately emerging as a trailblazer in the industry.

In the embryonic stages of its existence, AZoom encountered the customary challenges associated with a fledgling startup. The Siddiq family, spearheaded by the entrepreneurial acumen of its founders, navigated the intricacies of securing funding, cultivating strategic partnerships with car manufacturers, and meticulously laying the groundwork for a robust technological infrastructure. Their unwavering commitment bore fruit as AZoom officially rolled out its services, heralding the advent of a new era in car rentals.

From its inception, AZoom distinguished itself by prioritizing the curation of a diverse and meticulously maintained fleet of vehicles. The Siddiq family astutely recognized the importance of catering to a broad spectrum of customer preferences and needs. Whether it be a nimble compact car for urban commuting or a spacious SUV tailored for a family road trip, AZoom endeavored to offer the perfect vehicle for every conceivable occasion.

A pivotal juncture in AZoom's storied history materialized with the unveiling of its user-friendly mobile app in 1996. This groundbreaking application revolutionized the way customers engaged with the car rental process. By enabling individuals to seamlessly browse, select, and book their preferred vehicle from the convenience of their smartphones, AZoom not only streamlined the reservation process but also solidified its reputation as an industry innovator, setting new benchmarks for customer engagement.

As AZoom gained momentum, it harnessed cutting-edge technology to further elevate the rental experience. The introduction of keyless entry and ignition through the mobile app marked a transformative milestone. Customers could effortlessly unlock and start their rental vehicles with just a few taps on their phones, obviating the need for physical keys. This not only augmented convenience but also positioned AZoom as an environmentally conscious enterprise, contributing to sustainability efforts by reducing reliance on traditional keys. The company's unwavering commitment to transparency and fair pricing became a defining attribute of its operations, further cementing its reputation in the market. AZoom's app provided customers with exhaustive details about each vehicle, encompassing specifications, features, and pricing. This commitment to openness empowered customers to make informed decisions, fostering trust and setting AZoom apart from its competitors. As AZoom charts its course into the future, the Siddiq family remains at the helm, steering the company toward continued innovation and customer-centric excellence. Embracing emerging technologies and refining services to meet the dynamic demands of the modern traveler, AZoom Car Rental stands as a beacon in the industry, shaping the way people perceive and experience car rentals since its inception in 1996.
